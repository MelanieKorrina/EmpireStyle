What is Empire Style?

		Empire Style offers an innovative solution for the daily hassle that comes with picking out the 
		perfect outfit every morning. It allows you to upload images of your clothing to a virtual closet 
		which is organized into neat categories, so you can quickly scroll through your clothing to find 
		an outfit quickly and efficiently. Empire Style's most defining feature, however, is its random outfit generator. 
		If you're running low on time, Empire Style will automatically generate a perfectly suited outfit for 
		you to walk out with confidence!
